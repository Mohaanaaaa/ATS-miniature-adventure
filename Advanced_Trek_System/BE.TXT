Step 3: Understanding the Data Flow
Before we move to React, let's visualize how the "Swap" and "Storage" we discussed actually happens in this code:

The Registration: When you call /api/start_trek, the database records that "Person X" is now carrying "Band 12345...".

The Breadcrumbs: Every time /api/ingest is called, the Telemetry table grows. Because we use trip.locations (a relationship), the backend can automatically find every single spot that specific person has stood on.

The Real-Time Check: The get_active_trekkers route gathers everything—the person's name, their current spot, and their entire history—and sends it as one clean package to your Dashboard.
