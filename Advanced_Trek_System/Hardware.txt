4. Important Assembly Tips

Antenna is Critical: Use the 915MHz or 868MHz antennas compatible with your LoRa modules for maximum range.
GPS Cold Start: The Neo-6M GPS module may take up to 5 minutes to acquire satellites on first power-up. Test outdoors for optimal performance.
Wiring Checks: Ensure correct wiring—TX of sensors to RX of ESP32, and vice versa.


This comprehensive list provides a clear overview of the hardware you need and their estimated costs, ensuring a smooth assembly process for your advanced trekking tracker system.

Note : One Final "Advanced" Tip for Assembly:
When you buy the RYLR998 LoRa modules,
 ensure you get the ones that match your country's frequency (915MHz for Americas, 868MHz for Europe/India). Using the wrong frequency isn't just inefficient—it can be illegal in some regions!
